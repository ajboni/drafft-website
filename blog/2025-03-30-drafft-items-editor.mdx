## What Is the Items Editor?

The Items Editor allows you to **dynamically create and manage structured data** using multiple formats:

- **Plain text**
- **JSON**
- **HJSON**
- **TOML**
- **YAML**

This means you can work with **unstructured or semi-structured input** while still ensuring that your data remains usable. When exporting, Drafft will validate and convert the content into JSON, ensuring **consistency and compatibility** with your game project.
